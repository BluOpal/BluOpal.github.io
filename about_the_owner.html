About the Owner

My name is Laura Ganser and I am the owner of Blue Opal.

My Goals.

Who does not want to have fine jewelry at affordable prices? I do, but I noticed that every time I go to different stores looking for a real gem necklace, earrings, or bracelets, the prices were expensive ranging from $50.00 or more. With that in mind, I decided to starting making my own semi-precious stone ornaments. Quickly I realize that a nice piece of jewel can be made for a fraction of the retail price. Moreover, I noticed that while making it, I discovered it is my passion and I really enjoy creating pieces of necklaces that are classy and can be used for any occasion. That is when I started my own company back in 2019.

My goal is that every woman who does not have the budget to buy expensive jewelry can find it at an affordable price. All my jewelry is handmade using sterling silver and semiprecious stones like agate, moonlight, jasper, quartz, amethyst, topaz, citrine, turquoise, among others. When I am crafting, I always pay attention to every detail and ask myself, “Would I use this piece. How often?”

My future plan is to expand my business, make my products more widely known, include more pieces of jewels, rings, and earrings to my collections.
